# Best Online MSW Programs

Online MSW programs prepare students for advanced social work practice, leadership roles, and clinical licensure.

## Why Accreditation Matters
Students should always choose CSWE-accredited programs to ensure licensure eligibility in most states.

## What to Look For

- Cost per credit
